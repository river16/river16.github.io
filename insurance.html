Insurance and Waiver Information

You must register with USA Hockey for insurance. Please visit USA Registration and print the confirmation page. (If the link does not work, copy this text into your browser: https://www.usahockeyregistration.com)

The fee for USA Hockey membership is $40.00 and is valid from Sept 1, 2015 through August 31, 2016. USA Hockey Membership Benefits include access to educational resource materials for players, special discounts for apparel/merchandise, and, most importantly, insurance coverage for excess accidents, catastrophic accidents and general liability. You will also receive a subscription to the USA Hockey Magazine.
